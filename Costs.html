How much can you really save with green energy?

You can save at least 30% of what you pay to install your installation, and that is only the federal government benefits through 2034, there is still the benefits and subsidies you could be getting from your state, county, and even municipal governments for playing your part in saving the environment. Many green energy types will also have some sort of benefits again depending on where you live, making you money back on your taxes back years later, helping this investment pay itself off!
